What are Emotional Support Animals?

Emotional support animals are pets that provide comfort, reduce anxiety, and enhance overall emotional wellbeing. They are trusted companions for individuals facing mental health challenges, offering a unique form of therapy through their unconditional support. Drawing on guidelines from trusted sources like the APA and NAMI, our information is both accurate and supportive.

7 Benefits of Emotional Support Animals

  • Emotional Stability: Help reduce anxiety and depression through constant companionship.
  • Stress Relief: Lower stress levels by reducing cortisol through calming interactions.
  • Encouraged Physical Activity: Routine walks and play promote a healthier lifestyle.
  • Social Connection: Act as natural ice-breakers to foster social interaction.
  • Enhanced Mood: Boost overall mood with their calming presence.
  • Therapeutic Support: Serve as a complementary part of mental health treatment plans.
  • Sense of Purpose: Provide daily routine and responsibility, uplifting self-esteem.
